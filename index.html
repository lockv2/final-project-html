A church for today

Nouvelle Vie Church is located in the city of Longueuil and within Greater Montreal. Every week we gather with the purpose of exalting, worshiping and proclaiming the name of our glorious God, and all in French.



Nouvelle vie exists so that every person can
discover the love of God.
