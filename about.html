Who Are We



OUR DNA

We believe that the Christian Church must be in the image of Jesus. Like him, we are here to love, serve, and bring a message of hope to our world. This is the vision of New Life Church, a church for today.



“It was out of love that Jesus gave his life for humanity. His teaching inspires us to love God and to love our neighbor.”




OUR MISSION

We are passionately dedicated to establishing an influential church in Quebec and the Spanish-speaking world.
