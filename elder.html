ELDERS

The mission of the Elders Ministry is to reach seniors in order to share and communicate the love of God to the large church family. All year round, several days of activities are organized to socialize and get to know each other better. You can join us on our Facebook page or for those who need support with their funeral preparation, do not hesitate to contact us.
