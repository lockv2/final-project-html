NOUVELLE VIE MISSIONS

Nouvelle Vie Missions regularly mobilizes humanitarian, medical or construction teams to provide aid to populations in great need in the four corners of the planet. During these humanitarian trips, a host of emergency interventions and sustainable development initiatives are set up: houses, orphanages, churches and schools are built or renovated, food and medical care are provided. offered, and income-generating activities are created.
